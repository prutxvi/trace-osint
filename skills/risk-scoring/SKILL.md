# Skill: Risk Scoring

## Purpose
Assess exposure and risk levels based on verified public-source findings.

## Scoring Factors
- Number and type of public data points
- Sensitivity of exposed information
- Source reliability and corroboration
- Recency of findings
- Specificity of matches

## Risk Levels
- **Low (0.0-0.29)**: Minimal public footprint
- **Medium (0.3-0.49)**: Some public information available
- **High (0.5-0.69)**: Significant public exposure
- **Critical (0.7+)**: Extensive public information with sensitive data points

## Output
Exposure assessment with:
- Overall score (0.0-1.0)
- Risk level classification
- Contributing signal factors
- Human-readable summary

## Constraints
- Only assess based on verified public findings
- Never infer or speculate about private information
- Flag any borderline assessments as uncertain
