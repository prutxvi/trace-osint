# Skill: Reporting

## Purpose
Generate polished, structured investigation reports in multiple formats.

## Output Formats
- **Markdown**: Human-readable report with tables and formatting
- **JSON**: Structured evidence file for programmatic use
- **Audit Log**: Complete trace of investigation actions
- **Run Log**: Execution log with tool activity

## Report Sections
1. Executive Summary
2. Investigation Scope
3. Findings with Evidence
4. Entity Resolution
5. Source Inventory
6. Confidence Distribution
7. Risk & Exposure Assessment
8. Gaps & Limitations
9. Recommended Next Steps
10. Audit Trail
11. Policy Compliance

## Quality Standards
- All claims must trace to source findings
- Confidence levels must match evidence weight
- Gaps must be explicitly stated
- Policy compliance must be confirmed
- Reports must be self-contained and reproducible
